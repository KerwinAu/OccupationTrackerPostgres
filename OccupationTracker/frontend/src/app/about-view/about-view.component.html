Gym Occupation Tracker Description

Status:

Displays real-time data of the current gym occupancy and status. Allows users to instantly assess the current situation and make informed decisions.

Week:

Presents a weekly average overview, showcasing the average occupation for each day. Enables users to identify trends and patterns over the course of the week.

Month:

Compiles data on a monthly basis, offering a detailed breakdown of gym occupancy. Includes a visual diagram illustrating the daily trends throughout the month.

Overview:

Archives all data entries from the oldest to the most recent. Facilitates a comprehensive overview of historical occupancy data for strategic planning.
